About Us

We are a group of four Computer Science students at Ryerson University in Toronto, Canada. We banded together to achieve our goal. In doing so, we created EZPlanner.

We at EZPlanner strive to provide you with the most easy and streamlined experience to plan and enjoy travelling without all the hassle and stress. Our inspiration for this project struck during quarintine for COVID-19. Though many yearned to travel, for the safety of everyone's health, we were advised to stay indoors and avoid any unnecessary travel. It was then that we decided to look forward to when the pandemic would be over, and create a web application which allows users to plan their trips with ease. The app lets users locate one another, pick destinations, iron out small details and make sure everything is ready for the big day. We wanted to share our passion of travel with the world and look to the future.
