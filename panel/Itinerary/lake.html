Details

Duration: 3 days.

Tour description

The three days guided trip in xxxxxxxx park, we offer you the opportunity to paddle your own canoes in pristine rivers and lakes, cook over a campfire, sit around the fire and chat with each other while enjoying some fire toasted marshmallows, and sleep in the deep forest with your families and friends. At here, you will embrace true nature by encountering deers, rabbits, bears, or even wolves and other wild animals. We try to keep our group sizes small in order to make sure you have a great experience exploring the wild, and also to ensure the safety of you and your families/friends. Our experienced guide will offer you all the essential equipment you need for camping as well as teach you all the camping and survival skills. You will learn to choose the location for setting up a tent, start a fire, find directions by using a map and a ompass, keep warm in the night. This is a trip for people who want to escape from the noises of ities and for whom wants to improve their survival skills.
